The Team


Omega Crypto Assets Ltd. Is a Bulgarian company, responsible for developing the Rhea project. It will be the legal entity behind the platform. The team currently consists of three members.

Detelin comes from an economic background. Having discovered the fascinating world of financial markets he started making a living from them. Working as an equity trader, he also developed a passion for cryptocurrencies. After this, he came up with the Rhea project with one goal in mind – to create a unique hybrid product.

Georgi also has an economic education and experience as an equity trader. His advanced knowledge in cryptocurrencies pushed him to work towards his private venture. He teamed up with Detelin to found the Rhea project.

Vesselin is an IT-specialist with a wide range of skills in programming. He joined the Rhea project to setup the technical aspects of the ICO and to later become the team leader of the new IT team.
