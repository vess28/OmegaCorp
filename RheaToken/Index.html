Rhea is a revolutionary crypto-equity hybrid token.
Through the Rhea Crypto20 - our capitalization weighted index, we will enable multiple ways of hedging, speculating and investing in the cryptocurrency market without holding any individual currency.
Use the Rhea token to buy and sell options on our platform or provide market liquidity for extra return.
Our token will provide a quarterly dividend to all token holders and market participants.

What makes Rhea stand out?

This chart shows how a 2-year simulation of the Crypto20. The sharp moves reflect the high volatility of the cryptocurrency market due to frequent major developments. Sharp drops occur at least once every 3 months. Investors have no way of hedging their portfolios when this happens. How do we solve this?

The Rhea platform will offer options on the Crypto20 index, which can be used to speculate or hedge. By buying and selling options, investors can monetize their broader view on the market, or vastly reduce their risk exposure.
